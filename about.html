About Evan


I'm a software engineer and cartoonist located in Denver, Colorado.

I graduated from the University of Denver in 2018 with my B.S. in Computer Science. I've been working as a developer in Denver ever since.


You can check out some of my old cartoon work on diadotgram. I made those posts while studying abroad in Barcelona.


You might catch me on the trail in Colorado, hiking, trail running, or riding my space horse. Check me out on Strava.



This site was created in 2020 to showcase my work and serve as a platform for sharing new content.

I hope you enjoy what I have to share!
